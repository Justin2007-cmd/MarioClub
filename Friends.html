Meet Mario's Friends


Here are some of Mario's most friends and their notable characteristics:




Luigi

Mario's younger brother. He's a taller and thinner than Mario, often depicted as more cautious and timid but very brave when it counts. He wears a green hat and overalls.





Princess Peach

The ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom. Known for her kindness, grace and leadership. Often the damsel in distress but also takes an active role in various adventures. She wears a pink dress and crown.





Yoshi

A friend dinosaur who helps Mario by giving him rides and using his long tongue to eat enemies and obstacles. Known for his loyalty and cheerful demeanor. Yoshi comes in various colors, but green is the most iconic.





Toad

A Loyal attendant to Princess Peach and a resident of the Mushroom Kingdom. Known for his mushroom-shaped head and blue vest. He's helpful, brave, and always eager to assist Mario on his quests.





Bowser

He's a large, powerful, and famous creature resembling a dragon-turtle hybrid. Known for his imposing size, spiked shell, and fiery breadth, Bowser's main goal is often to kidnap the Princess Peach and conquer th Mushroom Kingdom.
